Product designing involves imaginating, creating and iterating product that solves users problems and addresing specific needs in a given market

Product designers are basically problem solvers, they asses prospective customers habits, behaviours, frustrations, wants and needs and find ways to provide solutions for these problems. As a product designer, you're meant to be empathetic, understand users, their needs, analyze solutions, provide solutons and test them with real life users.
